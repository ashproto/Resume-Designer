04 / Set up in minutes

Two short setups. Then you're writing.

Set up your OpenRouter key

Optional — only needed for the AI features.

1
Create a free OpenRouter account
Sign up at openrouter.ai — no card required to start.
2
Generate an API key
From your OpenRouter dashboard, create a new key.
3
Paste it into Settings
Drop the key into Resume Designer's settings panel.
4
Pick a model
Claude, GPT, Gemini, and more — switch any time.
Your key stays on-device and is sent only to OpenRouter, only for the AI calls you trigger.

How to use it

From blank profile to polished PDF.

1
Build your master profile
Enter your history by hand, or import an existing PDF or DOCX.
2
Generate or tailor a variant
Paste a job posting and tailor your profile to that role.
3
Review the AI's inline diffs
Approve or reject every change before it lands.
4
Export to PDF
Pick one of 11 layouts and send a clean, multi-page PDF.
05 / Questions

The short answers.

Is it free?
Yes. The app is free and open source under AGPL-3.0. If you use the optional AI, you only pay OpenRouter for what you use — nothing goes to us.
Is my data private?
Yes. Resume Designer is local-first: your profile, resumes, and settings stay on your device. There's no account, no backend, and no telemetry.
Do I need the AI?
No. The AI assistant is entirely optional. Every other feature — layouts, import, export, variants — works fully without a key.
Which platforms?
A native desktop app for macOS and Windows, plus a browser build you can run anywhere without installing.
